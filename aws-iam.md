# aws-iam

## Key Concepts

### Users
End users - general people, members of an organization

### Groups

Collections of users

### Policies

Made of [[aws-iam-policy-document]]s "Policy Documents".
Formatted in JSON.
Give permissions for User/Group/Role to take actions within AWS.
